Yasou

Yasuo is a high-skill champion in *League of Legends*, known for his agility and precision. Using wind as his weapon, he dashes through enemies, blocks projectiles, and unleashes powerful whirlwinds. His ultimate, *Last Breath*, allows him to strike airborne opponents. Yasuo is challenging to master but highly rewarding for skilled players.
